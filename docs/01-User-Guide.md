## 1. Getting Started

### Visiting the Website

Open your web browser and navigate to:

> **https://vualiku-xp.web.app**

The homepage features:

- **Hero Section** — A stunning introduction with a "Book Now" call-to-action
- **Featured Operators** — Cards showcasing our partner eco-tourism operators
- **Interactive Map** — See where each adventure takes place across Vanua Levu
- **AI Travel Assistant** — A chat widget in the bottom-right corner to help plan your trip

### Navigating the Site

The main navigation bar at the top includes:

| Menu Item | What It Does |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Home** | Returns to the main landing page |
| **Explore** | Search and filter all available activities |
| **Booking** | Start the booking process |
| **Map** | View the interactive adventure map |
| **Directory** | Browse all operators |
| **Sign In** | Log in with your Google account |

## 2. Creating Your Account

### Why Create an Account?

Creating an account lets you:
- ✅ Save your booking history
- ✅ Earn loyalty tier rewards
- ✅ Leave reviews for activities
- ✅ Access your profile dashboard
- ✅ Receive booking confirmations via email

### How to Sign In

1. Click the **Sign In** button in the top-right corner of the navigation bar
2. A Google Sign-In popup will appear
3. Select your Google account or enter your credentials
4. You're in! Your avatar will appear in the navigation bar

### How to Sign Out

1. Click your **avatar** (profile picture) in the top-right corner
2. A dropdown menu will appear
3. Click **Log out**

> **Note:** You don't need an account to browse activities, but you will need one to complete a booking.

## 4. Booking an Adventure

### Step-by-Step Booking Process

**Step 1 — Choose Your Operator**

On the Booking page, you'll see all operators displayed as cards. Click on an operator to see their available activities.

**Step 2 — Select Activities**

Browse the operator's activities. Each activity shows:
- Name and description
- Price (per head or per night)
- Duration
- Available time slot (Morning, Mid-day, Afternoon, Evening, Overnight)

Click **Add to Basket** on any activity you'd like to book.

**Step 3 — Choose Your Date**

Select your preferred date using the calendar. The system considers:
- Seasonal pricing (peak season Jun–Sep may cost more)
- Early bird discounts (book 30+ days ahead for savings)

**Step 4 — Set Number of Guests**

Enter how many participants. Group discounts apply automatically:

| Group Size | Discount |
|-----------|---------|
| 1–3 guests | Standard price |
| 4–5 guests | 5% off |
| 6–9 guests | 10% off |
| 10+ guests | 15% off |

**Step 5 — Select Your Starting Point**

Choose where you're travelling from. This helps estimate travel times to the activity location.

Options include airports (Labasa, Savusavu, Nadi), ferry terminals, and major towns.

**Step 6 — Fill in Your Details**

Enter:
- First and last name
- Email address
- Phone number (optional)

**Step 7 — Review & Checkout**

Review your booking summary including:
- Selected activities
- Smart pricing breakdown (seasonal, group, and timing adjustments)
- Total price in FJD

Click **Proceed to Checkout** to complete payment.

## 5. Your Basket & Checkout

### The Basket

Your basket (shopping cart) appears in the top navigation bar with a count of selected activities.

- Add multiple activities from the same or different operators
- Remove items by clicking the ✕ button
- View your running total

### Payment

At checkout, you'll be prompted to enter your payment details. Payments are processed securely through **PayDock** in Fijian Dollars (FJD).

After payment, you'll receive:
- ✉️ A **booking confirmation email** with your details
- 📱 A **WhatsApp notification** (if enabled) with your booking summary

## 7. Reviews & Ratings

### Leaving a Review

After completing an activity, you can leave a review:

1. Navigate to the activity page
2. Scroll to the **Reviews** section
3. Select a **star rating** (1–5 stars)
4. Write your review
5. Click **Submit Review**

Your review helps other travellers make informed decisions and supports our local operators.

### Reading Reviews

Each activity card displays:
- Average star rating
- Total number of reviews
- Individual review comments with reviewer names and dates

## 8. Digital Waivers

Some activities require a safety waiver before participation. When digital waivers are active:

1. You'll receive a **waiver link** in your booking confirmation email
2. Open the link: `/waiver/[your-booking-id]`
3. Fill in your details (name, email)
4. Add emergency contact information
5. Note any medical conditions
6. **Draw your signature** on the signature pad (works on both desktop and mobile)
7. Check the agreement box
8. Click **Sign Waiver**

> **Note:** Digital waivers may not be available for all activities. If waivers are currently disabled, physical waivers will be provided at check-in.

## 9. Cancellations & Refunds

If you need to cancel a booking:

- Contact the operator directly using the details provided in your booking confirmation email
- Cancellation policies vary by operator
- Refunds (if applicable) are processed back through the original payment method
- Please allow 5–10 business days for refunds to appear
