# **App Name**: Vualiku XP

## Core Features:

- Homepage with Tourism Image: Display a visually appealing image related to Fiji tourism on the homepage.
- About Page: Provide information about The Meridian solutions company and its services for tour operators.
- Tour Operator Directory: List Fiji tour operators with links to their websites and a 'Book Now' button. Includes: Waisali Nature Experience, Vorovoro Island, Dromuninuku Heritage and Tours, Drawa Block Cooperative, and Vanualevu farmstay.
- Contact Page: Display contact information: Email: admin@vualikuxp.com, phone: (679) 7630785.
- Footer Contact Strip: Implement a black background strip in the footer with contact info.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Sky Blue (#87CEEB) to evoke a sense of serenity and connection to nature, reflecting Fiji's beautiful skies.
- Background color: Very Light Blue (#F0F8FF) for a clean and unobtrusive canvas, suggesting open space.
- Accent color: Gold (#FFD700) for CTAs and highlights, mirroring Fiji's golden sun and sand; ensures visual prominence and complements the cool hues.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a versatile humanist sans-serif, chosen for both headlines and body text.
- The overall layout will mimic the design reference and adhere to the reference's color palette.
- Use clean, modern icons for navigation and to represent different types of tours (nature, cultural, etc.).
